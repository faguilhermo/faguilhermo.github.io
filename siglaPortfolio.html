SIGLA Dashboard


Goal: Reduce the waiting time for the chemotherapy session, from the patient who is waiting for the release of a ward to the support that is in need of nursing or cleaning. In addition, provide data-based support to the hospital manager.

Solution: Through the weight of the infusion bag, the equipment is able to infer information that will tell the status of the ward (available, unavailable, awaiting nursing, cleaning, replacing) through the dashboard that is being shown on an iPad at the nursing center, in an application built entirely on SwiftUI. In this way, nursing does not need to be observing or relying on patient warnings, being able to focus on other activities and provide assistance only when informed on the dashboard. Therefore, the connection between the application in SwiftUI and the support equipment is made through the IoT protocol, MQTT.

Another point of extreme importance is the generation of reports through the data collected and some calculations. Thus, in the report tab it is possible for the manager to see how the nursing team is doing, see the cleaning team's performance and even the occupation level, helping him to make informed decisions for optimization.
